A long time ago in a small town far far away a few nerds got together to build a robot. The Titan Technos are a diverse group of 9th-12th graders with varying talents to meet the multi-faceted demands of the FIRST Tech Challenges.

We are a small team in a great, tight-nit community. We all play a role on our team, our school, and our community. We were a rookie team in the 2016/2017 school year; the Velocity Vortex challenge. That year we were incredibly surprised to make it to the state competition. The following year, we split into two different teams to compensate for the boost in popularity of the robotics program at our school; teams 12035 and 13957.

We quickly learned that FIRST wasn't all about making cool robots. It is about the community, the learing experiences, and showing the potential of STEM jobs and activities to kids of all ages. We have done all sorts of community outreach - supporting the Lego Leauge at our feeder elementary school, participating in our homecoming parade, and helping out at the local libraries' STEM days are just a few.
